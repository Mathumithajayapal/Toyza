Getting Started with PlayStudio

Four simple steps to bring the magic of Toyza PlayStudio to your child or classroom.

1

Choose an Age Group

Browse our collections filtered by age range — from baby sensory to tween STEM kits, every toy is matched to developmental stages.

2

Pick a Play Theme

Select from creativity, science, literacy, social skills, or outdoor play. Each theme comes with activity ideas and goals.

3

Order & Unbox Together

Toys arrive in joyful, sustainable packaging. The unboxing experience itself is designed to build excitement and anticipation.

4

Play, Learn & Share

Follow our guided play cards or scan the QR code for video tutorials. Share your moments with #ToyzaPlayStudio!
