Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

📦 How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days across India. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available at checkout. Free shipping on all orders above ₹1499!
🌿 Are your toys eco-friendly and safe?
Absolutely! All Toyza toys are crafted from sustainable wood, finished with non-toxic, child-safe paints, and packaged in recyclable materials. Every product is rigorously tested for safety.
🔄 What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy on all unused products in original packaging. Simply contact our support team, and we'll guide you through the process. No questions asked!
